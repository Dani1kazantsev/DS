Demons of the Twelve Moons

  • Muzan Kibutsuji

    Muzan Kibutsuji - the main antagonist and first demon capable of creating others who kill and eat people

  • Demons of the Twelve Moons

    The Demons of the Twelve Moons (十じゅう二に鬼き月づき) is a group of strongest demons under the command of Muzan Kibutsuji, characterized by the increased content of his blood in their bodies to increase strength

  • High and low moons

    The demons of the twelve moons are divided into higher and lower moons. The higher and lower moons in turn are divided into numbers from 1 to 6 (in descending order of strength)

  • Targeting demons

    Demon targets received from Muzan:

    • 1) Find the Blue Spider Lily (the key ingredient in the experimental medicine that made Mouzan a demon)
    • 2) Destroy Demon Slayers
    • 3) Find Nezuko Kamado
